InstantPlayer Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Welcome to InstantPlayer. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information Collection

InstantPlayer only collects the minimum information necessary to run the application:

  • Storage Permission: To access and play audio files on your device
  • Media Audio Permission: To access and play audio content
  • App Usage Data: Such as playback history and playlists (stored locally only)

Information Usage

We use the collected information only for:

  • Providing music playback functionality
  • Improving user experience
  • Saving user preferences

Information Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload any data to the cloud or share it with third parties.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and delete app-stored data at any time
  • Uninstall the app to completely remove all related data
  • Deny permissions (may affect some functionality)

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally only
  • Secure data storage methods
  • Regular app updates to fix potential security issues

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted in the app, and continued use of the app implies acceptance of the updated policy.
